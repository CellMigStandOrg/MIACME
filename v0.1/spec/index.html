Overview

How to contribute

This document is published by the Cell Migration Standardisation Organisation Minimum Information Working Group as a Working Draft, which is developed with and for the cell migration community. We welcome and appreciate contributions, and to make comments regarding this document and/or the associated reporting guideline, participation is possible in a variety of ways:

  • participate in the Cell Migration Standardization Organization workshops
  • report issues in our issues tracker
  • contact the Working Group via e-mail at: wg1-cmso-minimalrequirements@vib-ugent.be
  • provide comments in the MIACME live spreadsheet detailing the elements of the reporting guideline

Future plans

MIACME v0.1 has focused on identifying descriptors required to represent in vitro cell migration experiments. In the next steps, we will:
  • extend MIACME checklist to in vivo cell migration experiments
  • produce examples of MIACME-compliant descriptions of different types of cell migration experiments
  • inclusion of further requirements related to semantic artifacts
  • release new versions of the MIACME checklist after incorporating the feedback received
